Patents & Protected Designs

Engineering protection around the ideas that move the market.

Why Our Patents Matter

At Kool Concepts, innovation isn’t accidental—it’s engineered. Our patented and patent-pending settings are designed to give our retail partners a defensible edge: recognizable aesthetics, elevated perceived value, and differentiation that can’t be easily copied on the sales floor.

These protections allow our partners to build long-term programs around unique designs, knowing the core architecture is backed by formal intellectual property.

Note This page offers a friendly overview of some of our protected innovations. If you’d like full legal details— including patent claims and jurisdiction coverage—we’re happy to share them privately under NDA.

Key Protected Innovations

Stitch-Set Architecture

A dimensional “stitched” setting that binds past, present, and future.

Granted Patent Utility / Design Studs · Pendants · Rings

A proprietary setting structure using “stitch” elements to anchor a central stone between upper and lower plates—symbolizing past and future—while maintaining structural integrity and comfort for everyday wear.

  • Supports multiple metal combinations and stone sizes
  • Engineered for repeatable manufacturing and consistent QC
  • Highly adaptable across price tiers and categories

Seamless Love Cluster Setting

Multiple small stones, one continuous field of light.

Granted Patent Utility / Design Studs · Rings · Pendants

A cluster-engineering approach that eliminates visible gaps between stones, creating the illusion of a single, larger diamond while maintaining a smooth, continuous surface and comfortable profile.

  • Micro-setting geometry optimized for brilliance and durability
  • Flexible templates for round, oval, pear, and halo silhouettes
  • Ideal for “quiet luxury” storytelling and AUR step-ups

Encoded Personalization System

From letters and dates to discreet, wearable ciphers.

Patent Pending Design / System Bands · Stacks · Bars

A structured layout system that translates initials, names, and dates into modular dot-and-dash arrangements—using rounds, bars, and negative space to create legible yet discreet codes in jewelry.

  • Standardized spacing for clarity and repeatability
  • Scalable across single pieces and stackable sets
  • Supports multi-metal and mixed-stone executions

Modular Motif Framework

A shared architecture for symbolic, story-forward pieces.

Patent Pending Platform Rings · Bangles · Charms

A base-jewelry framework that allows interchangeable motifs—such as path symbols, celestial icons, or stitched elements—to be swapped, upgraded, and recombined without compromising structural strength.

  • Enables build-your-own programs and capsule drops
  • Designed for SKU efficiency with maximum visual variety
  • Works across sterling, vermeil, and fine gold assortments
