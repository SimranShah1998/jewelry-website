2025 Proposals

  • Bold Color Revival
  • Zodiac Jewelry
  • Mixed Metals
  • Statement Charms

Bold Color Revival

2025 marks the return of vibrant enamel and vivid stones—think cobalt blues, neon greens, and rich citrines. Designers are embracing bold palettes to inject energy and optimism into fine jewelry pieces.

Zodiac Jewelry

Personalization reigns in 2025. Customers want meaningful pieces, and astrology continues to dominate. Charms, cuffs, and pendants with zodiac glyphs, birthstones, and moon phases are top performers.

Mixed Metals

Gold and silver come together in harmony. 2025 embraces layered, stackable pieces where contrast is king—two-tone rings, interlocking bangles, and layered necklaces are everywhere.

Statement Charms

Large, meaningful charms are replacing delicate ones. Think heirloom-inspired medallions, oversized lockets, and bold motifs like eyes, hearts, and initials.
