The Stitch Collection

Where Love Weaves the Past into the Future.

The Stitch Collection celebrates the invisible threads that bind us—love, memory, legacy, and the moments that shape who we are. Inspired by a technique invented in-house, each piece features 14K gold wire “stitches” that symbolize connection. A gold plate above and below represents the past and the future, anchoring the design in time, while a diamond at the center symbolizes love—held securely between the stitches and plates.
